About ShoreSquad

We're on a mission to make beach cleanups the coolest way to spend your weekend. By combining cutting-edge technology with social connection, we're building a community of young environmental warriors who are ready to take action.

Our platform makes it easy to find cleanup events, check the weather, rally your friends, and track your impact. Because saving the planet should be fun, social, and totally doable.
